Yes! Every course has a free preview of the first lesson. Pro plans also include a 7-day free trial — no credit card required.

Do certificates expire? +
Never. Your certificates are permanent and can be shared directly to LinkedIn, included in your resume, or downloaded as a PDF.

Pro members can download any course via our iOS and Android apps for offline viewing. Downloaded content stays synced for up to 30 days.

We add 8–12 new courses every month, plus regular updates to existing courses to keep them current with industry changes.
